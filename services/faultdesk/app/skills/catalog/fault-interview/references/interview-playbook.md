# Interview Playbook

Common follow-ups:

- Cannot connect: ask when it started and whether all devices are affected.
- Slow connection: ask whether the issue occurs on wired or wireless connections.
- Router or ONU lamp issue: ask which lamp is off, red, blinking, or abnormal.
- Intermittent issue: ask frequency and time of day.

When symptom and onset timing are both known, prefer diagnosis over more questioning.
